## Introduction

Do you know the 'proper' way to behave during meals? And what would be the worst thing someone could do while eating? This episode is all about etiquette, what makes good table manners and what is considered rude! Phil and Pippa discuss this and teach you some new vocabulary.

## Quiz

According to BBC programme The Food Chain, what was the most common complaint from listeners around the world about eating manners in their country?

- a) people eating with their hands
- b) people eating noisily
- c) people burping at the table

_Listen to the programme to hear the answer._
