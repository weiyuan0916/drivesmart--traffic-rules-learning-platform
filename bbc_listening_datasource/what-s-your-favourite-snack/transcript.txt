5. Answer the quiz questions. Listen again or use the transcript to help you.
1. What is the name of an Argentinian bread snack made with cassava, cheese and
salt?
a) chipa
b) chips
c) chupa
2. Which idiom describes something which is exactly what you want or need?
a) hit the jackpot
b) hit the spot
c) hit the highlights
3. Which phrase describes a situation where there is only just enough money to
live?
a) money is tight
b) money talks
c) time is money
4. "Anna always does exactly what she wants and doesn't care what other people
think. She _____."
a) swims up the current
b) swims with the current
c) swims against the current
5. Which word is similar in meaning to the adjective 'affordable'?
a) expensive
b) inexpensive
c) basic
6. Which idiom means 'done while busy travelling or moving around, not at home'?
a) on the go
b) on the run
c) on the way

6 Minute English worksheet
What's your favourite snack?
Answers
3.
a. hits the spot – (idiom) is exactly what you want or need
b. affordable – not expensive; cheap enough that most people can buy it
c. money is tight – there is only just enough money available to pay for the
basics needed to live
d. on the go – (idiom) done while busy travelling or moving around, rather
than at home
e. mental clarity – state in which your mind is fully focused, active and
engaged, not dull or foggy
f. swim against the current/tide – (idiom) do or say things which are different
from most other people, because you do not mind being different
5. 1a, 2b, 3a, 4c, 5b, 6a

Teacher's notes
This worksheet is standardised so that you can amend and use it as appropriate
with your classes.
Some topics may be difficult for learners, especially those which could be
upsetting or cause offence. Please be mindful of this when using this worksheet,
and adapt it as necessary.
Extension activities
• Role-play an interview with an 'interviewer' and an 'expert' on this topic.
• Write a short story or article using the vocabulary from this episode.
• Read original articles on this topic.
• For homework: If it is available in your country, download and listen to the
original podcast these extracts were taken from.
