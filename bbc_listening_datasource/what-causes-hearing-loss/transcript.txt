What causes hearing loss?
Answers
3.
a. sense organs – specific parts of the human body (ears, eyes, tongue, nose
and skin) which allow you to experience the outside world
b. funnel – move or direct something through a narrow space
c. wear and tear – the damage that comes from ordinary everyday use
d. we're doomed! – (informal) used to say that we are destined to be defeated
or to fail
e. downward spiral – situation which continuously gets worse and worse
f. redundancy in the system – having more than one way to achieve an
outcome so that a backup method can take over if other methods fail
5. 1c, 2a, 3c, 4b, 5b, 6a
