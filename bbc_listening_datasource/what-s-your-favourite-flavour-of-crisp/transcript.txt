Answers
3.
a. you can't beat that – (idiom) something is the best and you won't find
anything better
b. snowball effect – situation where one action causes many other similar
actions to happen
c. personal preference – an individual's subjective liking or disliking for
something, based on their personal taste and opinion
d. part and parcel – (idiom) essential feature or element of something
e. go-to – relied upon to be the best thing for a particular purpose or
situation
f. guacamole – paste made of mashed avocado, lime juice and salt
5. 1a, 2b, 3c, 4c, 5c, 6a

Teacher's notes
