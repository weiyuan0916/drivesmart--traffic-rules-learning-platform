4. What did you learn that was surprising or new in this episode?
5. Answer the quiz questions. Listen again or use the transcript to help you.
1. What were the original 'junk playgrounds'?
a) pre-made swings and slides
b) bombsites left after World War II
c) American theme parks
2. What does it mean if something 'tends to' happen?
a) it happens often and will probably happen again
b) it rarely happens
c) it has never happened
3. "Real Madrid has won the European Cup more than any other club. They
are _____ the most successful football team in Europe."
a) by most
b) by amount
c) by far
4. What is a 'small place for hiding things in'?
a) den
b) hidey-hole
c) bombsite
5. Which informal phrase shows that you 'agree completely with what
someone has said'?
a) one whole percent
b) fifty percent
c) one hundred percent
6. Which phrase means 'seeing something only quickly, without considering it
carefully'?
a) first come first served
b) at first glance
c) blink first

Answers
3.
a. at first glance – seeing something for the first time, before having a chance
to consider it more carefully
b. hidey-hole – small place for hiding things in, or hiding yourself in
c. tend to (do something) – do something often and be likely to do it again in
the future
d. by far – by a great amount
e. one hundred percent – (informal) completely; used to emphasise that you
agree completely with a statement
f. coping strategies – psychological ways of managing the stress of
emotionally challenging situations
5. 1b, 2a, 3c, 4b, 5c, 6b

Teacher's notes
This worksheet is standardised so that you can amend and use it as appropriate
with your classes.

upsetting or cause offence. Please be mindful of this when using this worksheet
and adapt it as necessary.
Extension activities
• Role-play an interview with an 'interviewer' and an 'expert' on this topic.
• Write a short story or article using the vocabulary from this episode.
• Read original articles on this topic.

original podcast these extracts were taken from.
