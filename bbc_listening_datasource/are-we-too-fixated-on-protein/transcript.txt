6 Minute English worksheet
Are we too fixated on protein?
Answers
3.
a. cast your eye over (something) – look quickly at (something)
b. in abundance – in large quantities; more than enough
c. health halo – consumer perception of food which overestimates how
healthy an item is based on a single feature – such as being low in calories,
organic or high in protein – even if its overall nutritional value is poor
d. the tide is turning – (idiom) things are beginning to change
e. fibermaxxing – American social media trend where people intentionally
consume high levels of fibre by eating fibre-rich foods and supplements
for their health benefits
f. hack – a good solution or piece of advice
5. 1a, 2c, 3b, 4b, 5c, 6a

Teacher's notes
