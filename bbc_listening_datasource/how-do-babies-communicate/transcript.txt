Answers
3.
a. one hundred percent – used to emphasise that you agree completely with
something, or that it is completely right or wrong
b. jerky – quick and sudden; stopping and starting, rather than smooth-
flowing
c. intentional – planned; done on purpose
d. to the naked eye – as seen without specialist instruments or specialist
knowledge
e. love at first sight – (idiom) experience of starting to love someone as soon
as you see them for the first time
f. unhelpful cliché – overused phrase or comment (cliché) which has the
effect of creating negative or unrealistic attitudes
5. 1a, 2c, 3b, 4b, 5c, 6a

Teacher's notes
This worksheet is standardised so that you can amend and use it as appropriate

and adapt it as necessary.
