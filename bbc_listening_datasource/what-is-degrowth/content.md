## Introduction

Should we shrink the economy to save the planet? In this episode, Phil is joined by Hannah from the What in the World podcast to talk about an idea called degrowth.

## Quiz

In 2015, world leaders agreed to limit global temperature rises. How much did they agree to limit them to?

- a) 1.5C
- b) 2.5C
- c) 3.5C

_Listen to the programme to hear the answer._

## Vocabulary

| # | Word | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | degrowth | the economic idea that we need to use and make less stuff |
| 2 | element of | part of |
| 3 | beneficial | useful or good |
| 4 | fast fashion | clothes made cheaply and quickly so that people can buy them more often |
| 5 | underestimate | not understand the importance or size of something |
| 6 | feasibility | the likelihood that something can be achieved |
