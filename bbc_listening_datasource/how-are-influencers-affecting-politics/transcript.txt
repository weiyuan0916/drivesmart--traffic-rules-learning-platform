How are influencers affecting politics?
Answers
3.
a. turned off – uninterested; repulsed
b. super (+ adjective) – very, extremely, more than usual
c. shape the narrative – have a strong influence over how something is
presented or understood
d. branch out (into) – start doing something different from what you usually
do
e. pushback – negative reaction to something new that has happened
f. dig up – search for hidden or forgotten information, often details that
could damage someone's reputation
5. 1c, 2a, 3b, 4c, 5a, 6a

Teacher's notes
This worksheet is standardised so that you can amend and use it as appropriate
