5. Answer the quiz questions. Listen again or use the transcript to help you.
1. A 'list of all the things you would like to do before you die' is a _____.
a) to-do list
b) top 10 list
c) bucket list
2. "This new mobile phone contract will give you unlimited data and calls for
half the money you used to pay – it's really good _____."
a) buck for your bang
b) bang for your buck
c) pass the buck
3. What are 'pros and cons'?
a) advantages and disadvantages
b) causes and effects
c) trials and errors
4. What is 'puke' slang for?
a) visitor
b) vehicle
c) vomit
5. Which word means 'overly sweet, sentimental or insincere'?
a) coastline
b) saccharine
c) turbine
6. What refers to 'the most important or essential point of' something?
a) the nub of
b) the hub of
c) the sub of

Answers
3.
a. bucket list – list of all the things a person would like to do before they die
b. puke – (slang) vomit, sick
c. bang for your buck – (idiom) value gained in return for the money, time or
effort you spend doing something
d. saccharine – excessively sweet or sentimental and so considered insincere
or inauthentic
e. the nub (of something) – the most important or essential point (of a
situation, problem, etc.)
f. pros and cons – advantages and disadvantages
5. 1c, 2b, 3a, 4c, 5b, 6a

Teacher's notes
This worksheet is standardised so that you can amend and use it as appropriate
with your classes.
Some topics may be difficult for learners, especially those which could be
upsetting or cause offence. Please be mindful of this when using this worksheet,
and adapt it as necessary.
Extension activities
• Role-play an interview with an 'interviewer' and an 'expert' on this topic.
• Write a short story or article using the vocabulary from this episode.
• Read original articles on this topic.
• For homework: If it is available in your country, download and listen to the
original podcast these extracts were taken from.
